# WhosLow? 

A purely client-side Fabric mod built for the combat community. Adds extremely precise health-based glowing outlines to players you hit natively giving you tracking during chaotic engagements.

Designed specifically to tackle the messy logic of instant-respawn PvP modes (like Duels, KitPvP, and FFA), the mod permanently tracks your target's health state in memory. The instant they heal back to full health or drop dead, the outline completely disappears so your vision stays unobstructed until you deliberately restart the engagement.

## Features
- **Target Tracking:** The outline ONLY applies to players you have actively struck. *This is not a giant wallhack ESP for the entire lobby.*
- **Health Indicators:** The target's outline is dynamically colored depending on how their health stacks up to yours: Default Red (Lower Health), Yellow (Equal Health), and Green (Higher Health). Colors are extremely easy to tweak natively in Mod Menu.
- **Resets on Respawn:** Built with a bulletproof timestamp structure that intelligently purges dead or instantly-healed players out of memory the exact millisecond their engagement drops.
- **100% Client-Side:** Entirely cosmetic and totally safe.

## Supported Versions

- **1.21 through 1.21.4 (Cross-Version Range)**
